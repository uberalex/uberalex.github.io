Research

My main research interest is in content analysis for personalisation, making software fit the user's specific needs at a specific time. I have worked with technologies like Linked Open Data, Topic Modelling, Adaptive Hypermedia, and the web. I have applied this work in domains including Technology-enhanced Learning, Customer Care, and Digital Humanities.
