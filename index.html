Research

My main research interest is content analysis for personalisation—software that fits the user's specific needs and preferences. I have worked with technologies like Linked Open Data, Topic Modelling, Adaptive Hypermedia, Web Services & Web Applications. I have applied this work to Technology-enhanced Learning, Customer Care, and Digital Humanities.
